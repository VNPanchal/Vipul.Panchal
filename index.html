About Me

I am a researcher specializing in developing integrated experimental and computational frameworks that transform RNA‑ligand discovery from isolated successes into scalable and reproducible biophysics‑driven pipelines. My work combines high‑throughput biophysics, RNA structural mapping, and structure‑enabled virtual screening to systematically interrogate challenging RNA and protein targets relevant to infectious diseases, antimicrobial resistance, and rare genetic disorders.

Alongside RNA‑based drug discovery, I am deeply engaged in cheminformatics and computational approaches—including machine learning for bioactivity prediction, molecular descriptor analysis, and structure‑based design workflows. My interest lies in bridging experimental and computational strategies to accelerate therapeutic discovery.
